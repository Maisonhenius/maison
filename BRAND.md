# MAISON HENIUS — Brand Reference

> "Fragrance is not worn. It is lived."

A House of Memory, Emotion, and Timeless Craftsmanship.

---

## Brand Identity

### Manifesto
Maison Henius transforms scent into memory. Each creation is an emotional identity rooted in craftsmanship and storytelling. Each creation is not simply worn, but lived.

### Philosophy
"Memory over product. Emotion over visibility."

The Maison exists between contrasts: garden and desert, life and silence. This duality defines every creation.

### Origin Story
Inspired by the landscapes of Jordan, where fragrant gardens meet the mineral vastness of the desert, the house creates fragrances shaped by nature, memory, and exceptional raw materials. Each composition reveals a subtle balance of freshness, depth, and character. A fragrance that gradually unfolds and leaves a unique impression.

### Core Values
- **Elegance** — refined, sophisticated, never loud
- **Exclusivity** — not secretive, but selective; designed for the connoisseur
- **Warmth** — inspired by ochre sands, amber tones, golden heritage
- **Timelessness** — enduring, not bound to trends
- **Cultural Depth** — subtle references to heritage, travel, artistry; never cliches

### Tagline Candidates
- **"Essences Beyond Time."**
- **"Where Rarity Meets Refinement."**
- **"You live the memories."**

## Strategy

### Positioning
Modern niche perfumery with exceptional performance inspired by the Middle East with strong storytelling. Sits in the sweet spot of the niche market:
- Premium enough for luxury retailers
- Accessible enough to drive volume

### Key Pillars
1. Long-lasting fragrances
2. Modern luxury branding
3. Strong storytelling
4. Accessible niche pricing

### 3-Year Plan

| | Year 1: POSITION | Year 2: ACCELERATE | Year 3: GROW |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Goal** | Install as niche perfume house | Scale as global fragrance brand | Challenge top fragrance brands |
| **Product** | Position the brand, define identity | Define best sellers, introduce new lines | Sustain best sellers, add lines |
| **In-Store** | Personalization in flagship doors, permanent merchandising | Maximize visibility via retailer negotiations | Ongoing in-store excellence |
| **Distribution** | Selected: international/regional perfumery chains, flagship doors, travel retail | Expand to additional markets | Continue expansion to more markets |
| **Media** | Digital + PR, social media, website SEO | Step change: working media (digital + print), new PR/influencer strategy | Sustain high media investments, focus on pillars |

### General Strategy
- Build brand awareness and equity in the region
- Recruit, engage, and educate customers
- Build consumer loyalty
- Create irresistible in-store and digital experiences
- Set up distribution to all niche perfume stores; increase productivity per door
- Invest in merchandising, brand agents, sampling, and training
- Build exclusivity and scarcity through selective distribution
- Invest in high-end marketing (advertising, collaborations, campaigns)

### Current Markets
- **Jordan** (origin)
- **UAE** (primary expansion)

### Website Objectives
- Establish brand awareness reference
- Build high-value luxury perception while maintaining accessibility
- Drive e-commerce through strategic sales campaigns
- Build loyal customer community; capture and manage contact data
- Leverage data analytics to guide campaigns and decisions

## Tone of Voice

### Writing Style
- **Narrative & Poetic** — sentences flow like storytelling, inviting readers into a journey
- **Evocative Language** — sensory words connecting to touch, light, warmth, memory (e.g., "amber glow," "golden silence," "textured elegance")
- **Confidently Minimal** — words chosen with precision; avoid unnecessary complexity
- **Inclusive Yet Exclusive** — speak to the "few who understand," but in a welcoming, not arrogant way

### Vocabulary

**Use:**
elegance, refinement, artistry, craftsmanship, amber, ochre, gold, warmth, timeless, enduring, essence, memory, emotion, connection, journey, creation, harmony, resonance, rare, sophisticated, cultivated, noble

**Avoid:**
sacred, ritual, confidential (suggests secrecy/spirituality), trendy, flashy, hype, affordable (mass-market terms), magical, mysterious, forbidden (overused perfume cliches)

### Channel Guidelines

| Channel | Style | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Website / Brand Book** | Longer sentences, narrative tone | *"Maison Henius was born at the meeting point of heritage and artistry, where amber tones of the desert meet the elegance of cultivated creation."* |
| **PR** | Concise yet sophisticated | *"Maison Henius embodies refined craftsmanship, inspired by a founder who has lived across continents and cultures, transforming memories into enduring fragrances."* |
| **Social Media** | Short, poetic, impactful | *"Amber glass. Golden columns. A fragrance that endures."* / *"To wear Maison Henius is to live the memory."* |

### Do's
- Evoke sensory luxury (sight: amber/gold, touch: textured paper, weight of glass)
- Emphasize craftsmanship (brass plates, zamac cap, textured packaging)
- Speak to emotion + memory connection
- Keep it poetic but precise

### Don'ts
- Use religious, ritualistic, or secretive language
- Sound mass-market or commercial
- Overpromise with exaggerated words ("the best," "unlike anything ever seen")
- Stack too many adjectives — sound curated, not overloaded

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **File:** `assets/images/logo.svg`
- **Type:** Ornate monogram with classical column motifs and circular border
- **Color:** Gold `#E9DB90`
- **Design elements:** Spiral volutes (Ionic column references), decorative crown elements, diamond markers flanking "MAISON HENIUS" text
- **Rules:** Maintain clear space. Never distort or add effects. Elegant serif wordmark with MH monogram.

### Color System

**Primary Palette:**
| Color | Usage |
|---|---|
| Black | Text, deep backgrounds |
| Ivory / Cream | Light backgrounds, label plates |
| Gold `#E9DB90` | Logo, accents, embossing |

**Secondary Palette:**
| Color | Usage |
|---|---|
| Sand | Warm backgrounds, neutral tones |
| Rose | Soft accents |
| Olive | Earth tones, natural textures |

**Packaging Palette:**
| Color | Reference |
|---|---|
| Ochre-Red | Canyon sandstone texture (see `assets/pictures/Jordan Landscape/Texture and color of the box.jpg`) |
| White | Clean contrast |
| Gold | Cap, label plate, embossing |
| Deep Amber | Bottle lacquer gradient (dark at top, golden-amber at base) |

All tones must be muted and refined. Evoke warmth of imperial sands and richness of oriental materials.

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Serif typeface (used on bottle labels and brand materials — classic, elegant)
- **Body:** Sans-serif typeface
- **Spacing:** Wide, generous letter-spacing
- **Weights:** Light
- Overall feel: airy, elegant, unhurried

### Visual Language
> "Luxury is what is felt, not shown."

- Soft, warm lighting (amber/golden glow — consistent across all product photography)
- Natural textures (travertine stone, sandstone, leather, wood)
- Minimal composition with architectural elements
- Subtle patterns and warm gradients
- Photography: muted, tactile, never glossy or flashy
- Product shots always on travertine/limestone pedestals (Jordanian stone aesthetic)

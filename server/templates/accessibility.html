Accessibility Statement

True luxury is inclusive

At Maison Henius, we believe that true luxury is inclusive. Our commitment to craftsmanship, beauty, and storytelling extends to the digital experience we offer to every visitor — without exception.

We are dedicated to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities, and that everyone can explore our world of fragrance with ease, comfort, and confidence.

Our Commitment to Accessibility

We strive to provide an online experience that reflects the same attention to detail and excellence found in our creations. Our website is designed with accessibility in mind and follows recognised international best practices.

We aim to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as established by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), to support accessibility for users with visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive differences.

WCAG 2.1 · Level AA

Accessibility Features

Our ongoing efforts include:

  • Clear, structured content for improved readability
  • Compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation
  • Optimised contrast and scalable typography
  • Descriptive text alternatives for visual elements

We continuously refine our digital experience to ensure it remains inclusive, elegant, and intuitive.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an evolving journey. We regularly review our website and implement enhancements to meet changing standards and user needs, ensuring an elevated experience for all visitors.

Contact & Assistance

If you encounter any difficulty accessing our website or wish to share feedback, we warmly invite you to contact us at info@maisonhenius.com.

We are committed to responding promptly and providing assistance whenever possible.
